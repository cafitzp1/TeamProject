ABOUT SLICKABLES

 

Home of the $2.50 ice cream sandwich! Slickables Ice Cream Sandwich is a family owned, family run dessert shop dedicated to offering great products at great prices. Our specialty is the ice cream sandwich which we sell for $2.50. You can choose any two freshly baked cookies and any flavor premium ice cream and we'll make it into a sandwich for you! Slickables - "Sandwiches That Are Lickables." What will be your favorite slickable? Also available: brownie sundaes and milkshakes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Some of the most common questions that our loyal customers ask. Feel free to contact us if you have any other questions!


 

Who are we?

We're are a small, cheery ice cream parlor here to satisfy your sweet tooth by combining the two greatest treats - Ice Cream and Gourmet Cookies!

 
 

What do we do?

We serve hand-made Gourmet Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiches to local neighborhoods of students and families. We work hard to provide a variety of ice cream flavor and cookie combinations to provide each customer a tasty experience.

 
 

Where are we?

Currently, we have three locations - Tempe, AZ (Arizona State University, Mesa, AZ (Downtown Mesa), and Riverside, CA (University Village).

 
 

When are we open?

Our Tempe location is open daily, from 11am - midnight.
