Get to Know Us Better!

Here are some of the most common questions that our loyal customers ask. Feel free to contact us if you have any other questions!

We are a Seattle based company that focuses on reducing the stress associated with event planning. We are founded by a group of Arizona State graduates that understand the pressure behind searching for the perfect event venue and aim to reduce that stress.
We partner with Seattle companies to offer a variety of venue types, locations, and styles. We do the work so that you don't have to. Think of us as your one-stop-shop for venue search and reservation.
Currently, our services are only available in Seattle, Washington. However,our goal is to expand across the nation!
